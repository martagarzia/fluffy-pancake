Come try delicious pancakes at the yearly pancake fair in Bradford.

Make your own Pancakes

  • Ingredients
  • Preparation steps

Ingredients:

  • flour - 120g
  • sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • baking powder - 2 teaspoons
  • salt - a pinch
  • egg - 1
  • milk - 240 ml
  • toppings: maple syrup, banana, dark chocolate...

Preparation:

  1. add the flower in a bowl
  2. add the eggs, sugar, baking powder and salt
  3. add the milk slowly while mixing
  4. pour the pancake batter in a non-stick pan
  5. stack the pancakes on a plate and add your favourite toppings
